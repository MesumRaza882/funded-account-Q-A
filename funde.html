    Candles Strategy

  14. Support & resistance

    15. Support: An area where the price can stop falling and may reverse upwards (bullish trend).

    Resistance: An area where the price can stop rising and may reverse downwards (bearish trend).

  15. Bullish Pin Bar:

    16. Small body on top, long wick at bottom; shows strong buying from support zone.

  16. Bearish Pin Bar:

    17. Small body at bottom, long wick at top; shows strong selling from resistance zone

  17. Bullish Engulfing:

    18. Big green candle fully covers small red candle; strong reversal from support.

  18. Bearish Engulfing:

    19. Big red candle fully covers small green candle; strong reversal from resistance.

  19. Bullish Harami:

    20. mall green candle inside previous big red candle; possible reversal at support.

  20. Bearish Harami:

    21. Small red candle inside previous big green candle; possible reversal at resistance.

  21. Bullish Morning Star:

    22. candles: Red → Small candle → Big Green covers 50% of more first candle; bullish reversal from support zone.

  22. Bearish Evening Star:

    23. 3 candles: Green → Small candle → Big Red; bearish reversal from resistance zone.
