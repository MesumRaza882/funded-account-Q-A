Moving Average kya hai?

When a fast EMA crosses above a slow EMA, it signals a buy, and vice versa for a sell. I combine this with support/resistance, candlestick patterns, volume analysis, and backtesting.
Moving average aik technical indicator hai jo price ka data smooth karta hai taake trend ka direction pata chale. Main zyada tar 10, 20, 50, 100 aur 200 wali EMAs use karta hoon. Jab fast EMA slow EMA ke upar cross kare to buy ka signal milta hai, aur agar neeche cross kare to sell ka. What are forex pairs?
Forex pairs kya hotay hain?
Major pairs are the most traded currencies like EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, and USD/CHF that include USD.
Minor pairs don’t include USD, like EUR/GBP or EUR/JPY.
Exotic pairs: One major and one low-volume currency, like USD/TRY.
Commodities are physical goods traded on the forex market, like XAU (gold), XAG (silver), and crude oil, often paired with currencies, like XAU/USD.
Major pairs wo hain jin mein USD shamil hota hai jaise EUR/USD, GBP/USD.
Minor pairs wo hotay hain jin mein USD shamil nahi hota jaise EUR/GBP.
Exotic pairs: ek major aur ek kam volume wali currency hoti hai jaise USD/TRY.
Commodities jismani cheezen hoti hain jaise sona (XAU), chandi (XAG), aur crude oil, jo forex market mein currencies ke sath trade hoti hain jaise XAU/USD.

What is a breakout and how do you trade it?
A breakout occurs when price moves outside a defined support or resistance level with momentum. I use breakouts by identifying key zones and confirming the move with volume and trend direction using moving averages.
Breakout tab hota hai jab price kisi important support ya resistance level ko todta hai, zyada momentum ke sath.
Main pehle support ya resistance zones identify karta hoon, phir dekhta hoon ke volume increase ho raha hai ya nahi.
Saath hi main moving average ka use karke trend direction confirm karta hoon — agar sab kuch align ho to main breakout trade leta hoon.
Do you focus more on breakouts, reversals, or trend trading?
I mainly focus on breakouts within the direction of the trend. I use moving averages to find trend direction and confirm with volume. I avoid reversals unless there’s clear volume divergence and candlestick confirmation.
Mera main focus breakout trading par hota hai, lekin sirf jab woh trend ke direction mein ho.
Main moving averages ka use karke trend ka direction find karta hoon, aur volume ke zariye confirmation leta hoon.
Reversal tabhi leta hoon jab volume divergence aur candlestick pattern dono milte hain.
Can you explain volume divergence and how you use it?
Volume divergence happens when price makes new highs/lows but volume fails to follow. For example, if price makes a higher high but volume is lower than before, it shows weak buying pressure and may indicate a potential reversal.
Volume divergence tab hoti hai jab price naya high ya low banata hai lekin volume us move ko support nahi karta.
Misal ke tor par, agar price higher high banata hai lekin volume pehle se kam hai, to iska matlab hota hai ke buyers weak hain.
Ye aksar reversal ka signal hota hai. Main is signal ko candlestick confirmation aur support/resistance levels ke sath use karta hoon.
