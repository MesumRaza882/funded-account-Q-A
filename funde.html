    Candles Strategy

  9. Bullish Pin Bar:

    10. Small body on top, long wick at bottom; shows strong buying from support zone.

  10. Bearish Pin Bar:

    11. Small body at bottom, long wick at top; shows strong selling from resistance zone

  11. Bullish Engulfing:

    12. Big green candle fully covers small red candle; strong reversal from support.

  12. Bearish Engulfing:

    13. Big red candle fully covers small green candle; strong reversal from resistance.

  13. Bullish Harami:

    14. mall green candle inside previous big red candle; possible reversal at support.

  14. Bearish Harami:

    15. Small red candle inside previous big green candle; possible reversal at resistance.

  15. Bullish Morning Star:

    16. candles: Red → Small candle → Big Green covers 50% of more first candle; bullish reversal from support zone.

  16. Bearish Evening Star:

    17. 3 candles: Green → Small candle → Big Red; bearish reversal from resistance zone.
